HENGE MIAMI

Henge is proud to announce the grand opening of its first flagship store in the United States, located in the heart of Miami’s Design District. The 350-square-metre space celebrates Henge’s distinctive aesthetic and design philosophy, where unique materials and timeless craftsmanship blend seamlessly with contemporary, visionary design. The showroom is conceived as a true “design atelier”, offering architects, designers, and design enthusiasts an immersive experience into the world of Henge.

The collections on display include iconic pieces alongside new releases, presented within an interior concept defined by striking contrasts and a masterful balance of materials. Every piece is handcrafted in Italy using carefully selected materials and exclusive artisanal techniques. The opening of this flagship store represents a significant step in Henge’s international growth, as it continues to bring its vision of luxury and design to a global audience.
